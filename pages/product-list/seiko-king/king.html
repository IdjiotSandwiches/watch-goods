King Seiko SPB369

IDR 27.677.099

Inspired by traditional royal amulets, the silver dial evokes Kazuno deerss antlers, with a lattice pattern echoing the “silk pattern” of the 1960s King Seiko. As in the original King Seiko, the dial also features meticulously cut three-dimensional indices, including a separately carved 12 osclock marker, and a date calendar.
